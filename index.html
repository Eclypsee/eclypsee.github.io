Hello, I am Evan, and here is my resume

I’m currently a second-year Computer Engineering student at CalPoly , with a concentration in robotics and mechatronics. From a young age, I’ve been captivated by how things work, always eager to take apart gadgets and explore the inner workings of electronics and hardware. As I continued to grow, I expanded my curiosity into coding, creating a Luhn algorithm validator as one of my first personal projects. I also experimented with soldering and spent countless hours flipping through IEEE magazines that were always lying around the house. My passion grew even stronger in high school, where I developed a deep interest in the ocean and marine biology, particularly through my experience in AP Biology. This led me to explore the fascinating world of underwater robotics in the MATE ROV competition , combining my love for technology with a desire to understand and protect marine environments. Today, I’m proud to be on a path in engineering, aspiring to become a skilled roboticist with a passion for innovation, especially in the realm of underwater exploration and marine technology.
